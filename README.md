# Apple-Project


Apple Inc. is a leading global technology company with 147,000 employees and key locations in Cupertino, California; Austin, Texas; Cork, Ireland; Shanghai, China; and Hyderabad, India. It is recognized for its diverse workforce and innovative products, particularly the iPhone, which has led the smartphone market for over a decade. Apple's product portfolio includes Macs, iPads, Apple Watches, and services like Apple Music and Apple Pay, all praised for their design and user experience. The devices support various network topologies, facilitating seamless connectivity and file sharing through technologies like iCloud and AirPort routers.
